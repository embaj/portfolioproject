Assignment 2: Face Generator

This project involved creating an interactive face generator using p5.js, where features like eyes, mouth, and shape dynamically change based on user inputs such as mouse clicks or movements. I explored the use of randomization and parameters to generate playful and unique variations of a face.

Assignment 3: Experimental Clock

This project involved designing an experimental clock that tracks time in unconventional ways, focusing on subjective or environmental factors rather than standard units like seconds or hours. My final design, the "Ambient Light Clock," reflects the passage of time through changes in ambient light, offering a more natural and experiential way to perceive time.

Assignment 4: Exquisite Corpse

This project involved collaborating with peers to create a playful "Exquisite Corpse" figure using p5.js, where randomized head, body, and leg parts combined to form unique characters. The final design featured a dynamic, slot-machine-style effect that added an engaging and unpredictable element to the process.

Assignment 5: Optical Illusion

This project involved designing an animated optical illusion in p5.js, featuring smoothly rotating rectangles that create a wave-like effect. By experimenting with rotation speed and time-based functions, I achieved a visually engaging and fluid animation that showcases the mesmerizing nature of optical illusions.

Assignment 6: Data Portrait

This project involved creating an animated data portrait in p5.js, visualizing my daily routine over a week through a flower-like design. Each petal represented a day, with circles for activities sized by their importance and animated to show the repetitive and structured flow of my routine.

Assignment 7: Autobiographical Game

This project involved designing an autobiographical game about my two dogs, showcasing their journey of meeting for the first time and learning to share their lives together. Through playful challenges and lighthearted sibling rivalry, the game reflects the emotional journey of adjusting to a new family dynamic.
